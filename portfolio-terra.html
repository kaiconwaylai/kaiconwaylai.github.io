Dashboard for Health Data

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Terra
  • Project date: June 2022
  • Project URL: https://github.com/kaiusconwaylai/TerraDemo
  • Technologies: MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS, TerraAPI

This was a group consultancy project developed over the summer term of my 3rd Year of university. We were given a client for whom we needed to produce a tangible product and ours took the form of a dashboard which could display and extrapolate information from health data provided by a user's wearables (e.g. MyFitnessPal, Garmin, FitBit). To do this we needed to use the API provided by Terra. It allowed me to improve my web development skills and gave experience with integrating another company's technology into my own.
